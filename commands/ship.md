Pre-flight checks and GO/NO-GO decision — verify everything before shipping.

**Usage:**
- `/ship` — Ship active feature
- `/ship <feature-name>` — Ship specific feature

**Pre-flight:** All tasks [x], tests pass, no Critical findings, conventions met

**Output:** GO/NO-GO with evidence (Correctness, Security, Performance assessment)
